WHAT IS A KABARET BANDUNG?

Cabaret is a performing arts that is a blend of theater art, dance art and artistic art wrapped through audio mixing with the concept of performance art that develop from time to time dynamically and very exist developed by young people in the city of Bandung. The art of cabaret performances has been recognized by the government as a new cultural arts in the city of Bandung with a modern nuance that has earned several awards one of which is the Cultural Award of Bandung in 2016.



Acting

Acting (role) is derived from the word "to act" which means "acting" acting in the sense he is a combination of body attraction and spiritual.

Dance

Dance is a human gesture that is completely separated from the elements of space, and time, and energy.

Artistic

Artistic value is the value seen in terms of its beauty in the cabaret seen in the background aspect, costumes and make up of players who have a role.
