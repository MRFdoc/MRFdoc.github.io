WHAT IS A KABARET?

Kabaret is a performing arts that is a blend of theater arts, dance art and artistic art that is very exist developed by young people in Bandung City.



Acting

Acting (role) is derived from the word "to act" which means "acting" acting in the sense he is a combination of body attraction and spiritual.

Dance

Dance is a human gesture that is completely separated from the elements of space, and time, and energy.

Artistic

Artistic value is the value seen in terms of its beauty in the cabaret seen in the background aspect, costumes and make up of players who have a role.
