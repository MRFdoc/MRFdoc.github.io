HISTORY OF KABARET BANDUNG.






Forum Kabaret Bandung


Forum Kabaret Bandung itself Established in 1999, The dominance of children cabaret from Stepu (Studio Kabaret Puragabaya) and only lasted for 1 year, Vacuum 8 Years, At Last Kabaret Forum Bandung Back in birth Year 2008, with early association Anton Yustuan (AAP) Windi, Wendi, Tarmizi, Seetonk (Badalohor) Nanang, Dani (Potret 19) Ihra, Soboer, Dedi, Triana, Dani Igu, Ivan, Warid (Stepu) and Deni (Trilogi). Finally FKB Officially Stood On May 10, 2008 At Dago Tea house Bandung.
